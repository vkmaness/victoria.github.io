About Me

Hello and welcome to my website!

My name is Victoria Maness and I am from Dallas, TX. I am currently on the job market for a new role in digital marketing. In the past, I have held the positions of Marketing Coordinator at Washington Prime Group (REIT), Social Media Manager at Beauty Cuts (hair salon), and Marketing Intern at The Artist Evolution (marketing agency); Through these positions, I gained experience in event marketing, graphic design, and various areas of digital marketing (for more, please check out my resume).

I am an artist at heart and I find excitement in pursuits that combine the creative process, strategy, and technology--all of which are essential parts of marketing. I knew I wanted to choose this career path when I had my first taste of marketing in the real world-- In undergrad, I was assigned to work with a local business in one marketing course and a nonprofit in another marketing course to help develop their marketing campaigns. My teams from both classes worked on a wide range of elements within a marketing campaign (ranging from market research, to creative briefs, to graphics, to experiential and social media marketing plans), and I recall how stimulating the process was; this experience was enriching and catapulted my interest!

Other things about me aside from marketing is that I like to create art, play with my cats, explore nature and food spots, lift weights, and travel (last two dates pre-pandemic unfortunately!). If you'd like to chat about similar interests and/or marketing, please feel free to shoot me an email or message on LinkedIn! :)
